Final Update

Hi, everyone Today it's heart breaking to announce that we the Creator/Founders of FelineGames are officially stepping back from this Community and shutting down all services including Projects. while we will continue to update projects that are not feature ready yet, we plan to Archive/Discontinue Everything by March 1st, 2026. Our supportive staff FrankGeorgie and Small Volunteers are welcome. Some of my Beta Testers that includes Herobrine and Hyperreal are real thanks. It's first important to acknowledge that money is finite and here it makes the most crucial decision. We don't have enough Man Power and Resources including Capital to continue funding all our Non-Profit Open-Source projects that don't benefit in either way (Neither Respect nor profit) We as FelineGames were always passionate about creating projects that benefit others and in someway bring in Donations and respect from others, which we miserably failed at (REAL TRUTH AND BITTERNESS) The Studio has unfortunately Shutdown, It's revival may not be Guaranteed!
